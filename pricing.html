What's Covered

Maintenance vs. new projects, spelled out

On the Managed plan, everyday changes are included. Bigger, brand-new features are quoted separately — and always agreed before any work starts, never surprise-billed.

Included in your plan

  • Text edits — hours, prices, contact info, wording
  • Swapping or adding photos
  • Posting a promo, announcement, or seasonal banner
  • Minor layout and section tweaks on existing pages
  • Fixing anything broken; uptime, security, backups

Quoted separately (a new project)

  • Online store, booking, or payment functionality
  • User logins, custom forms, or databases
  • Integrations — CRM, APIs, third-party tools
  • Full redesigns or rebuilds
  • Net-new pages that add real capability
